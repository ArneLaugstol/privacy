Who we are

WikiSpots is a mobile application. This policy explains how the app handles information.

SharpSW AS, Kyrresvei 24, Stabekk, Norway

What the app does

WikiSpots displays places and opens related Wikipedia articles. The app does not create user accounts and does not request personal information from you.

Data collection

We do not collect personal data from users and we do not store personal data on our servers.

We do not use in‑app analytics, advertising SDKs, or our own backend to track you.

Third‑party services

Although WikiSpots does not collect personal data, the app loads content from third parties. When your device requests this content, those services may receive technical information such as your IP address, device and app details, timestamps, and diagnostics needed to provide the service. These requests are made directly from your device.

  • Maps (Android): The app uses Google Maps SDK for Android to display maps and markers. If you enable “My location”, Google may process location data to provide the feature. See the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Articles: Wikipedia articles are served by Wikimedia Foundation services. Standard web request data (e.g., IP address) is processed to deliver pages. See the Wikimedia Privacy Policy.

We do not control how these third parties use information they receive under their policies.

Location

Location is optional. If you enable the device’s location feature (e.g., “My location” on the map), your location is used only to show relevant map content around you. The app does not store your location.

Data retention

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not retain it. Technical logs that are part of platform defaults (e.g., store crash reports) are handled by the respective platforms under their policies.

Security

All network requests from the app are made over encrypted connections (HTTPS), where supported by the service.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently interacted with a child’s personal data, please contact us so we can address it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
